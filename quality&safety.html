Quality & Safety

We prioritize maintaining excellent quality standards throughout our entire product line.

From the initial stages of product development to the final point of sale, quality is ingrained in every aspect of our operations.

Our Commitment to Quality Standards

& Best Practices

Testing

Aktachem's products undergo rigorous testing protocols prior to release. They are further tested by independent health authorities and laboratories in multiple countries to ensure compliance with regulations before reaching the market.

Manufacturing

We advocate for regular oversight of our manufacturing facilities by global government agencies. This reinforces our unwavering commitment to safety, the customers we serve, and the quality of our products.

Management

Quality is a fundamental right for every individual and a shared responsibility for all our employees. We have implemented a robust quality management system, including standards and procedures that all our employees and business partners must adhere to.

Regulatory Compliance:

At Aktachem, we actively engage in regulatory inspections to maintain the highest standards across our development, manufacturing, and distribution of herbs, botanicals, and nutraceuticals. We recognize the importance of inspections for nutraceuticals manufacturing facilities, and thus, support FSSAI initiatives to enhance inspection techniques. Furthermore, we advocate for increased factory inspections to contribute to the welfare of society.

In addition to internal and third-party inspections for quality assurance, we regularly arrange inspections by foreign health agencies to obtain approval when entering new markets.
